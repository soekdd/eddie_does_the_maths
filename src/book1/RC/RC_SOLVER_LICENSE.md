## Roux and Thistlethwaite support

`RC_RouxMethodSolver.mjs` uses the first-block solver from
[`cube-solver`](https://github.com/torjusti/cube-solver).
`RC_ThistlethwaiteSolver.mjs` uses its MIT-licensed cubie move model; the
four subgroup searches and pruning tables are implemented locally.
