Forum Rules & Netiquette

Welcome to the forum of [Name of the forum / operator]. To keep discussions pleasant for everyone, the following rules apply.

By using this forum, you agree to these rules.

1. Respectful Conduct

  • Treat other users in a friendly and respectful way.
  • Criticism is allowed, personal attacks are not.
  • Insults, provocations, discrimination, or hate speech are prohibited.
  • Stay on topic and contribute to a factual discussion.

2. Prohibited Content

  • No illegal, insulting, defamatory, or hate-inciting content.
  • No glorification of violence, threats, or calls to commit crimes.
  • No pornographic content or content harmful to minors.
  • No spam, misleading advertising, or chain messages.
  • No publication of third-party personal data without their consent.
  • No content that violates copyrights or trademark rights.

3. Posts and Responsibility

  • Each person is solely responsible for their own posts.
  • Make sure your information is accurate and does not harm anyone.
  • Use a clear title and write in an understandable way.
  • Please do not post intentional misinformation or trolling.

4. Moderation and the Operator's House Rules

  • The operator and moderation team exercise virtual house rules.
  • The operator reserves the right to edit, move, lock, or delete posts in whole or in part at any time.
  • The operator may close topics.
  • There is no entitlement to publication, restoration, or justification of individual moderation decisions.
  • In case of rule violations, measures may be taken without prior notice.

5. Reporting Violations

  • If you notice a problematic post, please report it to the moderation team.
  • Please do not escalate publicly - contact us directly.

6. Privacy and Public Visibility

  • Please note that forum posts may be publicly visible.
  • Do not publish sensitive personal data (e.g. address, phone number, bank details).
  • Further information can be found in our privacy policy.

7. Changes to the Rules

  • The operator may update these forum rules at any time.
  • The currently published version applies.
